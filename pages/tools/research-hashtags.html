The Research Hashtags tool combines real-time web data gathering with AI analysis:

  1. Your AI assistant sends parameters to the MCP server
  2. The server gathers real-time data from the web
  3. AI analysis processes the data into actionable insights
  4. Results are returned directly in your AI conversation

Everything happens in seconds, right inside your AI workflow. No browser tabs, no copy-pasting.
